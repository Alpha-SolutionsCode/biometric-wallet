## Getting Started

### What is Biometric Wallet?

Biometric Wallet is a secure digital payment platform that uses your fingerprint for authentication. It allows you to manage multiple currencies, cryptocurrencies, and perform peer-to-peer transfers with enterprise-grade security.

### Key Features

- **Fingerprint Authentication**: Secure login and transaction verification using biometric data
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Manage fiat currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.) and cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)
- **Peer-to-Peer Transfers**: Send money instantly to other users
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Track your portfolio performance with detailed charts
- **Cryptocurrency Integration**: Deposit and withdraw Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other cryptocurrencies
- **Transaction History**: Complete record of all transactions with export capabilities
- **Email Notifications**: Receive alerts for all account activities

### System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Device with fingerprint sensor or compatible biometric hardware
- Internet connection
- Email address for account recovery

## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the Biometric Wallet website
2. Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
3. Enter your email address
4. Complete the Manus OAuth authentication
5. Set up your fingerprint:
   - Click "Add Fingerprint" in settings
   - Follow the on-screen prompts
   - Place your finger on the biometric sensor
   - Confirm successful registration

### First Login

1. Enter your email address
2. When prompted, place your registered finger on the biometric sensor
3. You'll be logged in and directed to your dashboard

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Add your full name
3. Verify your email address
4. Set your preferred currency
5. Configure notification preferences

## Wallet Management

### Creating a Wallet

1. Go to **Wallets** section
2. Click **"Add Wallet"** button
3. Select the currency you want to add:
   - **Fiat Currencies**: USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD
   - **Cryptocurrencies**: BTC, ETH, LTC, XRP
4. Click **"Create Wallet"**
5. Your wallet is now ready to use

### Viewing Wallet Details

1. Go to **Wallets** section
2. Click on any wallet card to view:
   - Current balance
   - Wallet address (for cryptocurrency)
   - Transaction history
   - QR code for receiving funds

### Wallet Address

Each cryptocurrency wallet has a unique address for receiving deposits:

- **Bitcoin Address**: Starts with "1", "3", or "bc1"
- **Ethereum Address**: Starts with "0x"

**Important**: Never share your private keys. Only share your public address.

## Making Transactions

### Sending Money to Another User

1. Go to **Dashboard** or **Wallets**
2. Click **"Send Money"** button
3. Fill in the transfer details:
   - **To User**: Select or enter recipient's username
   - **From Wallet**: Select source wallet
   - **To Wallet**: Select recipient's wallet
   - **Amount**: Enter transfer amount
   - **Description**: Optional note
4. Review the details
5. **Verify with Fingerprint**: Place your finger on the sensor
6. Confirm the transfer

**Transfer Fees**: Standard transfers are free. Cryptocurrency withdrawals may have network fees.

### Receiving Money

1. Share your username with the sender
2. They will initiate a transfer to your account
3. You'll receive an email notification
4. The funds will appear in your wallet instantly

### Viewing Transaction History

1. Go to **Transactions** section
2. View all your transactions with:
   - Transaction type (transfer, deposit, withdrawal)
   - Amount and currency
   - Date and time
   - Status (pending, completed, failed)
   - Description

### Filtering Transactions

Use the filter options to find specific transactions:

- **Type**: Filter by transfer, deposit, withdrawal, or exchange
- **Status**: Filter by pending, completed, failed, or cancelled
- **Date Range**: Filter by specific dates

### Exporting Transactions

1. Go to **Transactions** section
2. Click **"Export"** button
3. Choose format:
   - **CSV**: Spreadsheet format for Excel
   - **JSON**: Structured data format
4. File will download automatically

## Cryptocurrency Operations

### Receiving Cryptocurrency Deposits

1. Go to **Wallets** → Select your crypto wallet
2. Click **"Receive"** or **"Deposit Address"**
3. Your wallet address and QR code will be displayed
4. Share the address or QR code with the sender
5. Deposits typically arrive within:
   - **Bitcoin**: 10-30 minutes
   - **Ethereum**: 1-2 minutes

### Withdrawing Cryptocurrency

1. Go to **Wallets** → Select your crypto wallet
2. Click **"Send"** or **"Withdraw"**
3. Enter withdrawal details:
   - **Destination Address**: Recipient's blockchain address
   - **Amount**: How much to withdraw
   - **Description**: Optional note
4. Review network fees (displayed before confirmation)
5. **Verify with Fingerprint**: Place your finger on the sensor
6. Confirm withdrawal

**Withdrawal Times**:
- Bitcoin: 10-30 minutes
- Ethereum: 1-2 minutes

### Checking Cryptocurrency Prices

1. Go to **Dashboard** or **Wallets**
2. Current prices are displayed for all cryptocurrencies
3. Click on any price to see:
   - Historical price chart
   - 24-hour change
   - Market information

### Currency Conversion

1. Go to **Exchange** section
2. Select currencies to convert:
   - **From**: Source currency
   - **To**: Target currency
   - **Amount**: Amount to convert
3. Real-time exchange rate is displayed
4. Click **"Convert"** to execute (if available)

## Security Features

### Fingerprint Authentication

Your fingerprint is your password:

- **Unique**: Your fingerprint is unique and cannot be duplicated
- **Secure**: Biometric data is encrypted and never stored in plain text
- **Fast**: Instant authentication without remembering passwords
- **Convenient**: No passwords to forget or reset

### Managing Fingerprints

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. View all registered fingerprints
3. To add a new fingerprint:
   - Click **"Add Fingerprint"**
   - Follow on-screen prompts
   - Verify with existing fingerprint
4. To remove a fingerprint:
   - Click **"Remove"** next to the fingerprint
   - Confirm with fingerprint verification

### Transaction Verification

For high-value transactions, you may need additional verification:

1. Review transaction details
2. Place your finger on the biometric sensor
3. Transaction is verified and processed

### Security Alerts

You'll receive email alerts for:

- New device login
- Fingerprint registration
- Large transactions
- Account changes
- Suspicious activity

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes in a secure location

### Device Management

1. Go to **Settings** → **Devices**
2. View all logged-in devices
3. To logout a device:
   - Click **"Logout"** next to the device
   - Confirm with fingerprint

## Troubleshooting

### Fingerprint Not Recognized

**Problem**: Fingerprint sensor doesn't recognize your finger

**Solutions**:
1. Clean your finger and the sensor
2. Try different finger angles
3. Register additional fingerprints
4. Use email/password backup method
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Transaction Failed

**Problem**: Transaction shows as failed or pending

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify sufficient balance
3. Check recipient address is correct
4. Wait 5-10 minutes for status update
5. Contact support with transaction ID

### Cryptocurrency Deposit Not Received

**Problem**: Sent cryptocurrency but not received

**Solutions**:
1. Verify correct wallet address was used
2. Check blockchain explorer for transaction status
3. Wait for network confirmation (10-30 minutes for Bitcoin)
4. Verify transaction fee was paid
5. Contact support with transaction hash

### Account Locked

**Problem**: Account is locked after multiple failed attempts

**Solutions**:
1. Wait 15 minutes before trying again
2. Verify you're using correct fingerprint
3. Try different registered fingerprint
4. Use account recovery option
5. Contact support

### Performance Issues

**Problem**: App is slow or unresponsive

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection speed
2. Clear browser cache
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Try different browser
5. Restart application
6. Contact support

### Email Not Received

**Problem**: Not receiving notification emails

**Solutions**:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address in settings
3. Check notification preferences
4. Whitelist noreply@biometricwallet.com
5. Contact support

## Best Practices

### Security

- Never share your wallet address with untrusted sources
- Verify recipient address before sending large amounts
- Keep your device updated with latest security patches
- Use strong, unique email password
- Enable 2FA for additional security
- Regularly review account activity

### Transaction Safety

- Start with small test transactions
- Verify recipient information before sending
- Double-check cryptocurrency addresses
- Keep transaction receipts
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### Account Management

- Regularly update your profile information
- Review and update notification preferences
- Monitor connected devices
- Keep backup codes in secure location
- Update recovery email if needed

## Glossary

- **Biometric**: Physical characteristic used for authentication (fingerprint, face, iris)
- **Blockchain**: Distributed ledger technology used for cryptocurrencies
- **Cryptocurrency**: Digital currency secured by cryptography
- **Hash**: Unique identifier for a transaction on blockchain
- **Wallet**: Digital container for storing currency
- **Address**: Unique identifier for receiving cryptocurrency
- **Transaction**: Transfer of funds between accounts
- **Fee**: Cost associated with a transaction
- **Confirmation**: Blockchain verification of a transaction
